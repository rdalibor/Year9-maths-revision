Surface Area of Cones & Pyramids

Key Concepts

For a cone, use the formula:

Total Surface Area = πr² + πrl

Square Pyramid

Surface area = Base + (4 × Triangle area)

Find the slant height ($l$) first using Pythagoras if needed!
